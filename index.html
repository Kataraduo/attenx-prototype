AttenX

ADHD Productivity App Prototype

Project Overview

AttenX is an AI-powered productivity assistant designed specifically for individuals with ADHD. The app dynamically adapts to users' attention patterns, guiding them through flexible workflows that optimize focus and prevent burnout.

Personalized Experience

AI-driven onboarding that adapts to individual ADHD patterns and work styles

Focus Management

Gamified task-switching and focus cycles with dopamine-friendly micro-breaks

Progress Tracking

Visual tracking of focus sessions, energy levels, and achievements
